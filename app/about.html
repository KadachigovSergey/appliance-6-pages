About us

Taking appliance repair and service seriously, Magic City Appliance Repair provides a superior level of support and care to our customers. We take on both commercial and residential appliance concerns, delivering top-notch results in a quick and efficient manner.

If the valuable appliances at your home and business are not performing correctly, contact the pros from Magic City Appliance Repair. We are here to provide you with skilled, affordable services that occur on your schedule. We are here when you need us! Every one of our technicians is courteous and will show up in uniform. We also will provide you with an upfront pricing before any work begins so there are no surprises on your final bill.
